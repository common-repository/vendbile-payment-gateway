Accept cypto currency payments on your WooCommerce store using Vendible API.

== Description ==

Accept Bitcoin, BitcoinCash, PIVX, IOP and Litecoin payments directly on your store using Vendible payment gateway.

The Vendible plugin extends WooCommerce allowing you to take crypto currency payments directly on your store with our API.

== Installation ==

Please note, v1 of this gateway requires WooCommerce 2.6 and above.

= Automatic installation =

For Automatic installation, log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu and click Add New.

Click on the upload file options and search your computer for the downloaded zip file. The downloaded archive is the one received on your account setup porcess on the Vendible merchant portal.
